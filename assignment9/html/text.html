About Us

Welcome to Yorkshire Snooker Centre

Welcome to our great new centre for Snooker, Pool and Billiards. We are a brand new centre with brand new facilities based in Huddersfield, West Yorkshire. Through our new web site you will be able to find and contact us, see our membership and competition fee's and also apply for membership online. We are open 7 days till late, have a large comfortable bar area, Sky TV and offer a range of bar snacks and a main meal menu.

Our halls offer 25 full size snooker tables, 15 English pool tables and 8 American pool tables. Also we cater for 8 and 9 ball pool games. We have a range of accessories offered for sale including cues, cues cases, cue extensions, chalks, Elk Master and Blue Diamond tips and an assortment of other accessories. We hope you will pay us a visit here at Yorkshire Snooker Centre and become a valued member for years to come.

We also a number of snooker and pool teams were we are involved in a number of local leagues. Come along and get involved, you never know where our next champion willcome from!
